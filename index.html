Edge Lighting App

Customization Tool for Android Devices

About the App

Edge Lighting App is a mobile customization tool that allows users to enhance their device screen with dynamic and colorful edge lighting effects.

Key Features

  • Custom edge lighting colors and styles
  • Animated lighting effects
  • Works on lock screen and home screen
  • User-friendly interface
